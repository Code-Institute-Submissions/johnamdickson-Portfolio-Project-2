How to Play

  1. To begin, flip over two cards by clicking on them.
  2. Player one takes the first go.
  3. Turn two cards over by clicking on them.
  4. If the cards match you score a point and your turn keeps going.
  5. If you get it wrong your turn ends and the next player has a go.
  6. The person with the most matches wins.
  7. Restart game any time by pressing the New Game button
